About Us

The project team actually consists of just one person, as I adopt a new online persona at different stages, each representing a course of action or goal. Over time, my online identity has grown increasingly diverse, encompassing more than five distinct characters, each with its own unique traits. I feel like their stories could be compiled into a novel.

Currently, the most frequently used usernames are TlogyVine and ReShadow. TlogyVine symbolizes a vine with an unusual behavior. Vines climb, symbolizing my relentless pursuit of excellence through personal effort. Topology, in reference to topological sorting, signifies that I always tackle simple tasks first, gradually overcoming each challenge in turn. ReShadow represents the shadows of the past. During my high school years, I worked extremely hard, and I believe this name serves as a reminder to never slack off.
